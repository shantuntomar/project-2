ABOUT US

VISION

To develop the institute into a centre of excellence in education, research, training and consultancy to the extent that it becomes a significant player in the technical and overall development of the country.


MISSION


1.To meet the global need of competent and dedicated professionals.

2.To undertake R & D, Consultancy & extension activities which are of relevance to the needs of the mankind.

3.To serve the community by interaction on technical and other aspects of development.

VALUES


1.Humanity and ethics blended with sincerity, integrity and accountability.

2.Productive delivery supported by healthy competition

3.Efficiency and dynamism coupled with sensitivity

4.To nurture innovation and ability to think differently with rational creativity.

5.Appreciation of sustainable socio-cultural values and to feel proud to be a good professional contributing to the betterment of mankind and mother earth.
